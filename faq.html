Common Questions

1. How do I track my water intake?

Simply log each glass of water you drink, and the app will automatically calculate the total number of glasses you've consumed throughout the day.

2. Can I set a daily goal?

Yes! You can set your personal daily water intake goal and the tracker will help you stay on target.

3. Why is hydration important?

Proper hydration helps maintain optimal physical performance, mental clarity, and overall health. It supports digestion, circulation, and regulates body temperature.

How It Works

Our app makes it easy to track your water intake by providing an intuitive interface. You'll log each glass of water you drink, and the app will show your progress throughout the day.

Water Intake Tips

Here are some tips to stay hydrated:

  • Carry a water bottle with you throughout the day.
  • Drink a glass of water before each meal.
  • Set reminders to drink water regularly.
