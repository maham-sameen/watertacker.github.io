Project Information

Water Tracker is more than just a hydration tool. It's a reminder to keep you on track with your health and wellness goals. Staying hydrated is vital for your body to function properly, and this website helps make it easier than ever to monitor your daily water intake.

By marking each glass of water you drink, Water Tracker will calculate your total consumption for the day. It also gives you feedback on your hydration habits and shows progress towards your daily goal.

Whether you're aiming for a specific target or just want to track your water consumption, this tool is designed to support your wellness journey.
