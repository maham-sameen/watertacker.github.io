Frequently Asked Questions

How can I track my water intake?

Simply log each glass of water you drink, and our tool will automatically calculate your daily intake.

Can I set a daily goal for water consumption?

Yes, you can set a personal goal, and the tracker will remind you if you're falling behind.

What happens if I forget to log my water intake?

We send friendly reminders to ensure you stay on track with your hydration goals!
